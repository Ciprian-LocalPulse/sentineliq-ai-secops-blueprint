# Public Edition Notice

The SentinelIQ AI SecOps Blueprint repository is published as a Public Edition landing and governance surface.

It is intentionally limited to public-safe product positioning, static assets, contact routes, and governance documentation. It is not a complete production implementation, enterprise transfer package, diligence room, certified security control, or deployed SOC platform.

## Included in the Public Edition

The Public Edition may include:

- Static product-description files.
- Public image assets.
- Public README and governance documentation.
- High-level capability and buyer-audience descriptions.
- Public support, security, contribution, and licensing boundaries.

## Excluded from the Public Edition

The Public Edition excludes:

- Private source logic and proprietary implementation details.
- Customer data, client environments, and deployment assumptions.
- Operational deception logic, detection rules, telemetry pipelines, or monitoring schemas.
- Internal prompts, private playbooks, response procedures, or business strategy.
- API keys, tokens, credentials, secrets, or private configuration.
- Commercial contracts, pricing, acquisition materials, and enterprise transfer records.

## Private Enterprise Edition

A Private Enterprise Edition, commercial blueprint package, or transfer package may be prepared separately for qualified commercial, acquisition, or diligence conversations.

That package may include private materials under the applicable platform terms or written agreement only. Nothing in the Public Edition grants access to those materials or creates a right to use them.

## Procurement-Safe Interpretation

The Public Edition is suitable for initial review of public positioning and governance posture. Any production, compliance, security, transfer, or acquisition assessment should be performed against the applicable private package and written commercial terms.
